# FlowLens — Product Thesis

FlowLens is a **Workflow Evidence & Process Intelligence Platform**, not a screen recorder.

The core loop: **Capture -> Understand -> Improve -> Route -> Measure.**

1. **Capture** the real, messy way work actually happens — not a scripted demo.
2. **Understand** it with AI: what was attempted, where the friction is, what's risky.
3. **Improve** the process by surfacing automation opportunities before they compound.
4. **Route** the same capture into an SOP, a bug report, a training guide, or a ticket —
   without redoing the work by hand for each audience.
5. **Measure** the time and money saved across every workflow that gets documented.

## Why this matters now
Steps Recorder is gone. Loom is a video box, not structured evidence. Scribe and Tango are
strong at step-by-step guides but stop at documentation — they don't diagnose friction, score
automation opportunity, or route findings into engineering/compliance systems. Nobody has
made the leap from "record a screen" to "understand and act on a workflow."

## The wow moment
Capture a messy workflow -> get a polished evidence pack -> friction findings -> automation
suggestions -> a generated SOP -> a generated ticket -> a training guide draft -> an estimated
time/money saved figure. All from one capture, in minutes, not a documentation sprint.
