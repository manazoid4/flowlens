# Buyer personas (priority order)

1. **COO / Head of Operations** — owns process consistency across departments; buys Business
   or Enterprise tier; cares about friction visibility and automation ROI reporting upward.
2. **Support / CX Lead** — owns ticket resolution time and agent onboarding; buys Team tier;
   cares about visual evidence and reducing repeat-ticket handling time.
3. **QA Lead / Product Manager** — owns bug quality and reproduction speed; buys Team tier;
   cares about structured reports engineers can act on without back-and-forth.
4. **IT Support Lead / MSP owner** — owns provisioning and troubleshooting knowledge; buys
   Team or Agency tier; cares about a reusable process library across technicians/clients.
5. **Compliance / Audit Lead** — owns evidence for SOC 2/ISO/internal audit; buys Business or
   Enterprise; cares about structured, timestamped, exportable evidence.
6. **Training / Enablement Lead** — owns onboarding quality; buys Team tier; cares about
   training guides that reflect how work is actually done today.
7. **Agencies / Consultants** — owns client reporting and process handoffs; buys Agency tier;
   cares about per-client workspace isolation and branded output.
8. **Founders / Builders** (lowest priority) — owns their own process before hiring; buys
   Founder tier; cares about cheap, fast documentation of their own workflow.
