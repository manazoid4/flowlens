# Category definition

FlowLens creates and owns the category **"AI Process Intelligence Layer"** (alt: "Workflow
Evidence Platform"). This sits a level above:

- **Video tools** (Loom) — storage and playback of a recording, no structure.
- **Screenshot/how-to tools** (Scribe, Tango, Guidde, Supademo) — good at generating a guide,
  but stop at documentation.
- **Bug/QA tools** (Jam.dev, Marker.io, Ybug) — good at capturing a bug, narrow scope.
- **SOP/knowledge tools** (Process Street, Trainual, Whatfix, WalkMe) — good at storing and
  enforcing a process once written, not at generating it from real work.

FlowLens's differentiation is that one capture becomes many assets, each grounded in an AI
diagnosis of friction, risk, and automation opportunity — positioning it as infrastructure
that operations, QA, support, IT, compliance, and training teams all plug into, rather than a
point tool any one of them uses alone.
