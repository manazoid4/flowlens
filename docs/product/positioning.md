# Positioning

**For** operations, QA, support, IT, and compliance teams whose process knowledge lives in
people's heads and forgotten video links,
**FlowLens is** an AI process intelligence layer
**that** turns one messy workflow capture into SOPs, bug reports, training guides, tickets,
and measurable ROI,
**unlike** screen recorders, how-to generators, and SOP documentation tools,
**FlowLens** diagnoses friction and automation opportunity and routes findings directly into
the systems teams already use.

## One-line pitches by buyer
- **COO / Ops**: "See exactly where your processes break down, and fix it without a
  consultant."
- **Support/CX lead**: "Turn every ticket resolution into reusable evidence and training."
- **QA lead / PM**: "Bug reports your engineers can actually reproduce, every time."
- **IT / MSP**: "Document a fix once, resolve the next ten tickets in seconds."
- **Compliance / Audit**: "Audit-ready evidence packs generated from real work, not memory."
- **Training / Enablement**: "Onboarding guides built from what your best people actually do."
- **Agencies / Consultants**: "Client reports without the manual writeup."
- **Founders / Builders**: "Document your own process before you have to hire for it."
