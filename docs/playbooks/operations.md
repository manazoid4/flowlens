# Playbook: Operations

**Goal**: turn a workflow that lives in one person's head into a living SOP.

1. Capture the workflow as it's actually performed (not the "ideal" version).
2. Open the AI Workflow Doctor and review friction + automation findings.
3. Generate the SOP export, edit for clarity, and publish to `/process-library`.
4. Re-capture quarterly (or after any system change) to keep the SOP current — a stale SOP
   is worse than no SOP, because it looks authoritative while being wrong.
5. Track the friction score over time for high-value workflows; a falling score is your
   evidence that process improvements are working.
