# Playbook: Founder

**Goal**: document your own process before you have to hire for it.

1. Capture yourself doing a task you know you'll eventually delegate (onboarding a customer,
   publishing content, closing the books).
2. Skip the polish — the AI Workflow Doctor and training-guide export do the tidying.
3. Hand the generated guide to your first hire on day one instead of a live shadowing
   session.
4. Revisit the capture's friction score before scaling the process — fix the friction before
   you bake it into someone's job description.
