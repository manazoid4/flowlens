# Pricing benchmarks (directional)

Not sourced from live pricing pages during this session — treat as directional estimates
for calibration, and re-verify before using in external sales materials:

- Loom Business: roughly low-teens USD per seat/month.
- Scribe/Tango team plans: roughly $20-40 USD per seat/month.
- Whatfix/WalkMe (enterprise digital adoption platforms): typically five to six figures USD
  per year, custom-quoted.
- Process Street/Trainual: roughly $25-50 USD per user/month at team tier.

FlowLens's per-workspace (not strict per-seat) pricing in `docs/product/pricing.md` is
positioned above video/how-to tools and below full enterprise digital-adoption platforms,
reflecting that its value driver is workflows-processed-and-routed rather than headcount
alone.
