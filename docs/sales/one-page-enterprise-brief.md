# FlowLens — one-page enterprise brief

**What it is**: An AI process intelligence layer that turns real, captured work into SOPs,
bug reports, training guides, tickets, and audit evidence — with friction scoring and ROI
measurement.

**Who it's for**: Operations, QA, Support, IT, and Compliance teams running processes across
multiple systems where knowledge currently lives in people's heads or scattered documents.

**Why now**: Windows Steps Recorder is gone. Video tools don't structure evidence. How-to
generators stop at documentation. Nobody diagnoses friction and routes findings into the
systems teams already use (GitHub, Jira, Linear, Slack, Notion).

**How it works**: Capture -> Understand (AI Workflow Doctor) -> Improve (friction/automation
scoring) -> Route (SOP/bug report/training guide/ticket) -> Measure (time/money saved).

**Security posture today**: Workspace-scoped access control, in-editor redaction tooling,
audit-evidence export format. SOC 2 Type II, SSO/SCIM, and customer-managed keys are roadmap
items — see `/security` for the honest current-vs-planned breakdown.

**Pricing**: Starter £120/mo, Team £450/mo, Business £1,200/mo (primary tier), Enterprise
custom, Agency £650/mo, Founder £19/mo. See `/pricing`.

**Proof point in this build**: a working demo at the deployed URL with six realistic seeded
captures spanning finance, QA, support, IT, compliance, and agency use cases.
