# Demo script (under 5 minutes)

Goal: land the "wow moment" — one capture becomes an evidence pack, findings, and multiple
generated assets — inside five minutes of attention.

1. **Open `/dashboard`** (30s). "This is Acme Operations. Every card here is a real workflow
   someone captured — not a demo script." Point at the friction and automation panels.
2. **Open a capture** (`/captures/cap-invoice-approval`) (60s). Scroll the step timeline.
   "Every screenshot is a real step, tagged and timed automatically."
3. **Open the AI Workflow Doctor** (`/captures/cap-invoice-approval/ai`) (90s). Walk through
   "what was attempted," friction summary, and findings cards. Land on the automation finding
   ("auto-extract invoice fields") as the single highest-impact insight.
4. **Open Export** (`/captures/cap-invoice-approval/export`) (60s). Show the markdown export
   already generated, and the list of additional formats (SOP, bug report, ticket, audit
   pack) that route from the same capture.
5. **Close on ROI** (30s). Point back at the dashboard's time/money saved tiles. "This is one
   workflow. Multiply this across every team that runs on process."

Have `/pricing` open in a second tab in case the conversation moves to budget.
