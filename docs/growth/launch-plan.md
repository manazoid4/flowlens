# Launch plan (concise)

**Phase 1 — private beta**: 5-10 design-partner teams from the priority persona list
(Ops, Support, QA) using their own real workflows, in exchange for shaping the roadmap.

**Phase 2 — comparison-led content launch**: publish the `/compare/*` pages and matching
short-form posts ("Windows Steps Recorder is gone — here's what replaces it") timed to
existing search demand rather than a big-bang announcement.

**Phase 3 — persona-led outbound**: target COO/Ops and Support/CX leads directly using the
one-page enterprise brief (`docs/sales/one-page-enterprise-brief.md`) and the demo script
(`docs/sales/demo-script.md`).

**Phase 4 — agency/consultant channel**: the Agency tier is priced and positioned for
consultancies to resell FlowLens-generated client reports; treat this as a distribution
channel, not just a pricing tier.

**Not yet done**: any actual outbound, paid acquisition, or partnership work — this plan is
directional for whoever picks up growth execution next.
